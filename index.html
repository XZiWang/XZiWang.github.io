Research

Research Interests

I am interested in algorithm design, machine learning and computational geometry, encompassing a wide range of topics. My experience lies in developing algorithms for handling large datasets, as well as working with online and streaming algorithms, robust optimization and their applications in machine learning, such as active learning and efficient training computation. Additionally, I have a strong inclination towards generative models, Bayesian inference, and leveraging deep models to tackle combinatorial optimization problems.
